MotionCoach is a basketball training app that uses your phone camera to automatically count makes and misses during shooting drills — you just prop your phone up facing the hoop, set a drill, and shoot. Using computer vision (YOLOv8), the app detects the ball and rim in real time and tracks whether each shot goes in, giving you live shooting percentage, rep counts, and session history so you can see improvement over time. The long-term vision is to pair this with a custom wristband that adds wrist biomechanics data and haptic feedback — so the app can not only tell you your shooting percentage but coach your form in real time — but the camera-based shot tracker is the first thing to build and validate with real players before investing in hardware.
